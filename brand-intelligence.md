It uses an original three-thread mark and Delivery Weave visual derived from the role's cross-practice mandate. The official logo sources are recorded for provenance and future authorized asset use.

## Visual intelligence

- **Palette:** deep navy and Azure-adjacent blues, bright cyan highlights, white/light-neutral fields, and restrained warm accents for focus states. Exact campaign colors are original employer-adjacent choices, not asserted as an official brand specification.
- **Typography character:** clean, modern, high-contrast sans serif; large confident headlines; compact uppercase labels; direct enterprise-technology tone. No official typeface is claimed.
- **Photography/illustration character:** human collaboration, enterprise technology, Azure delivery, and abstract technical systems; energetic but professional.
- **Geometry and interface motifs:** cloud contours, rounded cards, clear grids, strong horizontal structure, blue/cyan gradients, and modular practice architecture.
- **Overall vibe:** expert, optimistic, Azure-native, collaborative, technically credible, and delivery-oriented.

## Campaign application

- **Candidate site:** original Delivery Weave ribbons represent Data & AI, App Innovation, and Cloud Platform crossing shared delivery gates.
- **Resume and cover letter:** restrained navy/blue/cyan hierarchy with white paper canvas; no corporate-logo imitation.
- **Interview brief and worksheet:** same employer-adjacent palette with consulting-document density and explicit candidate-vision labeling.
- **Usage boundary:** The campaign is an independent candidate work product and does not imply 3Cloud or Cognizant endorsement.
