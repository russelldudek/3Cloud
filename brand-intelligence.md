## Typography evidence and decision

Official website CSS declares **IBM Plex Sans** most frequently, with **Roboto** also present. The campaign does not copy or commit proprietary or remotely served font binaries. It uses the declared family name first and a licensed/broadly available structural substitute stack:

`"IBM Plex Sans", "Liberation Sans", "Segoe UI", Arial, sans-serif`

This preserves the public site's geometric-humanist sans character, compact enterprise labels, strong weight contrast, and direct technical rhythm. The substitute stack is not described as official.

## Photography, interface, and geometry cues

3Cloud's public identity is expressed through enterprise-technology composition, dark ink fields, bright blue accents, modular cloud/AI diagrams, strong horizontal structure, clean grids, and optimistic technical motion. Official photography was not required to establish recognition because the official logo, real color system, typography evidence, and role-specific system diagram provide a clearer and more supportable campaign identity. No generic stock photography is used.

Original campaign visuals derive from documented company cues:

- three practice ribbons for Data & AI, App Innovation, and Cloud Platform;
- square technical gates and thin-grid architecture;
- dark ink / source-blue / sky-blue recognition layer;
- restrained warm decision signal used only inside the candidate operating metaphor.
