## Thesis

**AI delivery scales at the seams.**

The campaign argues that the role's real mandate is to turn cross-practice architecture into reliable AI delivery, then convert project learning into governed, reusable patterns that improve client value and delivery leverage at post-acquisition scale.

## Evidence integrity

The campaign does **not** claim multi-year Azure delivery tenure. It explicitly addresses that as the strongest reasonable hiring objection and relies on verified adjacent evidence: AI workflow design, agentic and retrieval patterns, evaluation/governance rails, cross-system operating transformation, high-reliability quality systems, and technical-business translation.

## Research and brand basis

The role thesis is grounded in the current 3Cloud job posting, 3Cloud's public acquisition announcement, MILO materials, AI services pages, and company positioning. Company-specific recommendations are labeled or framed as candidate hypotheses for discovery. Brand cues and official asset provenance are documented in `brand-intelligence.md`; the campaign uses an original role-derived visual system and does not present itself as an official company publication.
