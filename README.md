# 3Cloud Candidate Campaign

RoleForge candidate campaign for Russell Dudek's application to **Senior Consultant - AI** at 3Cloud.

## Thesis

**AI delivery scales at the seams.**

The campaign argues that the role's real mandate is to turn cross-practice architecture into reliable AI delivery, then convert project learning into governed, reusable patterns that improve client value and delivery leverage at post-acquisition scale.

## Campaign surfaces

- `index.html` - candidate vision site and interactive Delivery Weave
- `resume.html` - exactly two-page printable role-aligned resume
- `cover-letter.html` - exactly one-page printable cover letter
- `interview-brief.html` - role intelligence and interview thesis brief
- `delivery-seam-review.html` - one-page operating worksheet
- `styles.css` - shared responsive, accessible, print-aware visual system
- `app.js` - keyboard-accessible Delivery Weave tab interaction

## Evidence integrity

The campaign does **not** claim multi-year Azure delivery tenure. It explicitly addresses that as the strongest reasonable hiring objection and relies on verified adjacent evidence: AI workflow design, agentic and retrieval patterns, evaluation/governance rails, cross-system operating transformation, high-reliability quality systems, and technical-business translation.

## Research basis

The role thesis is grounded in the current 3Cloud job posting, 3Cloud's public acquisition announcement, MILO materials, AI services pages, and company positioning. Company-specific recommendations are labeled or framed as candidate hypotheses for discovery.
