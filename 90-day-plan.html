Success measures

  • Delivery: accepted increments, predictable handoffs, reduced integration rework.
  • Quality: explicit release evidence, escaped-defect signals, known failure modes.
  • Trust: clear authority boundaries, escalation ownership, reviewer agreement.
  • Adoption: actual workflow use, overrides, abandonment, and user feedback.
  • Reuse: qualified asset candidate with adaptation burden understood.
  • Learning: Azure-specific gap closure demonstrated through work.
90-day promise: enter with humility about platform-specific gaps, make the quality of decisions and handoffs visible, and earn broader scope through evidence.
