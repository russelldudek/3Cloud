## Asset-use decision

The current official `3Cloud - a Cognizant company` PNG is used locally and unmodified for nominative employer identification. It is paired with an immediate independent-candidate qualifier and is not placed in corporate navigation, legal furniture, or fake official letterhead. The campaign does not imply endorsement by 3Cloud or Cognizant.

No official photography is used. Company recognition is established through the official mark, source-sampled colors, evidence-based typography behavior, and original technical geometry derived from the public brand system. No generic stock imagery is substituted.
